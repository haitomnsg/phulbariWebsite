About Phulbari

Phulbari blends on-device AI with IoT to care for your plants. Identify over 100 flower species, detect 10 common leaf diseases, and water plants automatically with a smart ESP32-based system. An offline LLM assistant answers your plant questions even without internet.

Identify 100 Flowers

Powered by efficient Mobile AI (TensorFlow Lite), recognize flower species instantly with your camera.

Detect 9 Diseases

Spot leaf diseases early and get treatment steps and YouTube Video to cure.

IoT Auto-Watering

ESP32 + Moisture Sensor monitor soil and water your plant just in time.

Offline LLM Assistant

Ask questions about care, watering and sunlight. Works offline, respects privacy.
